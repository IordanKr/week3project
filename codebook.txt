Coursera Week 3 Course Project - Code Book
features: variable names, different values that were anlyzed
ytrain and ytest: activities that were analyzed,
1 WALKING
2 WALKING_UPSTAIRS
3 WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
4 SITTING
5 STANDING
6 LAYING

xtrain and xtest: observations for the test and training subjects

testfull: the testing dataset, with subjects, activities and observations
trainfull: the training dataset, with subjects, activities and observations

fulldataset: a merge of the training and testing dataset, hence having all the observations with renamed activity labels

tidydataset: the average of each observation of the full dataset looking at mean or standard deviation, grouped by subject and activity.
